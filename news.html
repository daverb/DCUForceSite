DCU Force is the mens rugby club of Dublin City University. We have three teams competing in competition, the 1st XV competes in Division 1 of the Irish Colleges league, the 2nd team competes in Division 3 and we also have a Fresher's team competing in the Fresher's division. As we are a University team players are representing DCU, therefore players can come from any club in Ireland and it does not effect their playing status.

Training normally runs on Monday evenings in the DCU Sports Grounds and league games take place on Wednesdays.

The club has developed immeasurably over the last number of years with the appointment of a rugby development officer and increasing our number of scholarship players from 4 to 8. A number of our past players have gone on to professional careers including Bernard Jackman and Jamie Heaslip to name but two.

As well as having players playing in the top tiers of Irish rugby we also cater for beginners who want to learn the core skills of the game and host regular beginners orientated training sessions throughout the college year.

Off the pitch the club has a busy social side with regular trips, events and nights out. It's a great place for students to come and meet some friends and get involved in extra curricular college life.

To find out a bit more information about joining the club, training times or anything else contact us on the club email address or any of our committee members and we'll be happy to provide more details.
