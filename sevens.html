DCU Force have a big appreciation of 7s rugby and feel the shorter version of the game has a big role to play in the development of players at every level.
In the last 3 years we have won the All Ireland Colleges Sevens twice and finished as runner up once. We also represented Ireland at the European University Sevens Championship where we were narrowly beaten in the semi final and coming third over all. As well as that we regularly enter invitational and regional club tournaments around Ireland.

For more info on our sevens team, training and competitions contact us through our contact page and we will give happily provide more details.
